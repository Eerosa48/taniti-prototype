FAQs

Frequently requested information for visitors.

Common Questions

Drinking age is 18.
Power outlets are 120 volts (same as the United States).
Alcohol may not be served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m.
Taniti has many native languages; English is commonly spoken in tourist areas.
There is one hospital and several clinics; the hospital has multilingual employees.
Violent crime is rare, though petty crime can occur as tourism increases.
